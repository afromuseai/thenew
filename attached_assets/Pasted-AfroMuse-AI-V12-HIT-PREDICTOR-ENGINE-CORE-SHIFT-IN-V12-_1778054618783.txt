🎯 2. HOOK ENGINE RULE (V12 STRICT)

A valid hook MUST have:

✔ 1 emotional core
✔ 1 rhythm bounce
✔ 1 repeatable phrase
✔ max 7 syllable cluster feel

❌ INVALID HOOK EXAMPLE:

“Yɛn nsa ahyɛ ase, yɛn ankasa na yɛbɛsan”

👉 Too long, too sentence-like

✔ FIXED HOOK:

“Yɛn nsa yɛ bom”
“Me gyidi kɔ so”
“Ɛnyɛ twerɛ bio”
